Bruce Bay Beans and Bunks

Formerly known as the Bruce Bay Coffee Cart, our family-owned business has transformed from a beloved mobile coffee spot into a charming, permanent haven. Here, we combine the warm hospitality of simple, comfortable lodging with the rich, inviting aroma of freshly brewed coffee. Whether you're a traveler seeking a restful night or a coffee enthusiast looking for your next cup, Bruce Bay Beans and Bunks offers a unique experience that feels like home. Join us and make your journey unforgettable!
